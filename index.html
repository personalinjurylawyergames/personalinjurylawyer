Snake Game

About

Welcome to the Snake Game! This game is a simple and fun implementation of the classic snake game. The goal is to eat the red squares and grow your snake as long as possible without hitting the walls or yourself.

p>In Snake Game, you control a growing snake that moves around the screen to collect food. The objective is to eat as much food as possible without colliding with the walls or the snake's own body. As the snake consumes food, it grows longer, making the game progressively more challenging.

Features

  • Simple yet engaging gameplay
  • Responsive design for seamless play on desktop and mobile devices
  • Smooth animations and intuitive controls
  • Increasing difficulty as the snake grows
  • No downloads required â€“ play directly in your browser

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to navigate the snake.
  • Collect food to increase your score and make the snake longer.
  • Avoid crashing into walls or your own body to stay in the game.
  • Challenge yourself to achieve the highest score possible!

Why Play Snake Game?

Snake Game is a timeless classic that tests your reflexes and strategic thinking. Whether you're looking for a quick break or aiming to beat your own high score, this game offers an enjoyable and rewarding experience for players of all ages.

Enjoy playing Snake Game and see how long you can survive!
